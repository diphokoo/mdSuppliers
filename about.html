About MD Suppliers (Pty) Ltd

MD Suppliers (Pty) Ltd is a 100% young black male-owned company, founded in 2020. The company was established out of a dire need to create sustainable employment opportunities and empower previously disadvantaged individuals. We are a construction company, specializing in:

  • Mining Geological Borehole Surveying
  • Civil Construction
  • Mine Construction
  • Supplying Mining Materials

Our mission is to foster growth by contributing to the country's economic mainstream, especially through job creation in previously disadvantaged communities.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to be the best known for quality, competitive pricing, and just-in-time delivery in the construction and mining industries. MD Suppliers aims to empower individuals through employment opportunities and ensure a sustainable future for our communities.

Founders

MD Suppliers (Pty) Ltd was founded by Given Madlalisa and Tshepo Diphoko. Both founders are passionate about creating employment and uplifting communities that were historically disadvantaged. They lead the company with strong values of integrity, commitment to quality, and a focus on empowering others.

Keys to Success

  • Comprehensive understanding of the project's requirements for success upon completion.
  • Strong technical skills and expertise to deliver high-quality construction projects.
  • Commitment to environmentally friendly practices and use of the highest-grade materials.
  • Effective staffing to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget.
